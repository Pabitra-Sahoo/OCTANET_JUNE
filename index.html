SIX PATHS OF PAIN

God Of The New World

The Six Paths of Pain was a jutsu used by Nagato Uzumaki with the help of Rinnegan. It allowed Nagato to control six different bodies as if they were his own. Each of these bodies was a reanimated corpse, which made use of Nagato's chakra. The Six Paths, as the series revealed, are called as follows: Deva Path, Asura Path, Human Path, Animal Path, Preta Path, and Naraka Path.All of them have different powers.With these power Pain destroyed the konha village with a single blow.

ALL PATHS

DEVA PATH

The Deva Path (天道, Tendō) has tha ability to manipulate attractive and repulsive forces with objects and people.

It has the ultimate jutsu Almighty Push.

ASURA PATH

The Asura Path (修羅道, Shuradō) has the ability to augment their own body to summon mechanised armour and various ballistic and mechanical weaponry.

HUMAN PATH

The Human Path (人間道, Ningendō) has the ability to read the mind of any target by placing his hand on the target's head or chest and yanking the soul out of the body.

ANIMAL PATH

The Animal Path (畜生道, Chikushōdō) has the ability of summoning various animals and creatures to aid it in battle. These creatures appear to be immortal.

PRETA PATH

The Preta Path (餓鬼道, Gakidō) has the ability to absorb chakra in any form through physical contact.

NARAKA PATH

The Naraka Path (地獄道, Jigokudō) has two main abilities: interrogation and restoration, by using the King of Hell.
